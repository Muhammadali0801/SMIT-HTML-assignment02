Zaika House Daal Varieties

Enjoy our flavorful and comforting varieties of lentils cooked to perfection!

  • Daal Mash

    Our Daal Mash is a creamy and comforting dish made with split yellow lentils cooked in spices, perfect for a warm and nutritious meal.

  • Daal Chana

    Our Daal Chana is a flavorful and hearty dish made from split chickpeas, simmered with a blend of spices for a rich and satisfying taste.

  • Daal Masoor

    Our Daal Masoor is a vibrant, spiced red lentil curry, full of flavor and cooked to perfection for a wholesome, delicious meal.

  • Daal Mong

    Our Daal Mong is made with yellow split moong lentils, cooked with traditional spices to create a light, healthy, and flavorful dish.
