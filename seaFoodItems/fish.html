Zaika House Fish Dishes

Deliciously prepared fish dishes, grilled, fried, and curried to perfection!

  • Grilled Fish

    Our Grilled Fish is marinated with aromatic spices and grilled to perfection. It's served with a tangy sauce and a side of fresh veggies, offering a flavorful experience!

  • Fried Fish

    The Fried Fish is crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, made with fresh fish fillets seasoned with a blend of spices, deep-fried for that perfect crunch!

  • Fish Curry

    Our Fish Curry is cooked in a rich, flavorful gravy with traditional spices. It's the perfect comfort food, served with hot, fluffy rice for a satisfying meal.

  • Tandoori Fish

    Tandoori Fish is marinated in a mixture of yogurt, spices, and herbs, then cooked in a tandoor for a smoky, charred flavor. It’s a favorite for those who love bold, smoky dishes!

  • Masala Fish

    Masala Fish features fish cooked in a rich, spicy masala gravy that’s packed with flavors. It’s a must-try for spice lovers looking for a zesty, tangy meal!

  • Steamed Fish

    Our Steamed Fish is delicately prepared with minimal spices, allowing the natural flavor of the fish to shine. It's a light and healthy option for those looking for a milder fish dish.

  • Fish Fry

    Fish Fry is a classic favorite, featuring fish fillets coated in seasoned flour, then shallow-fried for that golden, crispy texture. Perfect for a quick and satisfying meal!
