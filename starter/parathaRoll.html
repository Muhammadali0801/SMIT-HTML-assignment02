Zaika House Roll Paratha

Indulge in our special roll parathas, a perfect combination of flavors and spices!

  • Beef Boti Roll

    Our Beef Boti Roll features tender, marinated beef boti wrapped in a soft paratha, offering a flavorful and filling experience with every bite.

  • Chicken Boti Roll

    The Chicken Boti Roll is a delicious combination of juicy chicken boti, perfectly spiced and wrapped in a soft paratha, ideal for a satisfying meal.

  • Malai Beef Boti Roll

    Our Malai Beef Boti Roll brings a rich and creamy twist to the classic beef boti roll, with marinated beef pieces in a malai sauce, wrapped in a soft paratha.

  • Malai Chicken Boti Roll

    Our Malai Chicken Boti Roll features tender chicken pieces in a rich, creamy malai sauce, served in a soft paratha for a mouthwatering experience.

  • Tikka Boti Roll

    The Tikka Boti Roll is made with spiced, grilled chicken or beef tikka boti, giving you a smoky, flavorful bite wrapped in a soft paratha.

  • Seekh Kebab Roll

    Our Seekh Kebab Roll is made with juicy seekh kebabs, grilled to perfection and wrapped in a paratha, delivering a savory, smoky flavor with every bite.

  • Shami Kebab Roll

    The Shami Kebab Roll is filled with tender, spiced shami kebabs, giving you a mouthwatering taste wrapped in a soft, flaky paratha.

  • Paneer Tikka Roll

    For a vegetarian option, the Paneer Tikka Roll offers grilled paneer marinated in spices and wrapped in a paratha for a rich, smoky taste.
