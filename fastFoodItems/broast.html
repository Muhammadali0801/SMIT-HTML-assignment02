Zaika House Broast

Experience the crispy and delicious broast, served with our signature sauces and sides!

  • Chicken Broast

    Our Chicken Broast is made with perfectly marinated chicken, deep-fried to crispy perfection, and served with a tangy dip. It's a must-try for all fried chicken lovers!

  • Beef Broast

    The Beef Broast is a delicious twist, featuring tender beef marinated with spices, then deep-fried to crispy perfection. Served with a flavorful dip and fresh sides for an unforgettable meal.

  • Mutton Broast

    Our Mutton Broast offers a rich and flavorful experience, with juicy mutton marinated in aromatic spices, deep-fried to a crispy golden texture. A delightful choice for mutton lovers!

  • Spicy Broast

    The Spicy Broast features crispy fried chicken or beef with a spicy seasoning blend that packs a punch. Perfect for those who like a little extra heat in their meal!

  • Garlic Broast

    Our Garlic Broast combines crispy fried chicken or beef with a rich, garlicky coating. Served with a garlic dip, this broast is ideal for garlic lovers looking for a bold flavor.

  • Lemon Herb Broast

    The Lemon Herb Broast is made with a blend of fresh herbs and a zesty lemon marinade. It's a perfect choice for those who prefer a fresh and tangy twist to their fried chicken or beef.
