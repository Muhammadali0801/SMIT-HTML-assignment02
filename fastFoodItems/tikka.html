Zaika House Tikka

Experience the flavor-packed, smoky taste of our Tikka varieties!

  • Chicken Tikka

    Our Chicken Tikka is marinated with yogurt and spices, grilled to perfection. It offers a smoky, tender texture with a rich, savory flavor, perfect for serving with naan or rice.

  • Beef Tikka

    The Beef Tikka is made with juicy cuts of beef, marinated in a blend of spices and grilled until tender. It has a robust flavor and is a perfect choice for meat lovers.

  • Mutton Tikka

    Mutton Tikka is made with marinated mutton pieces, slow-cooked and grilled for a smoky flavor. The tender meat and rich spices will leave you craving more.

  • Malai Tikka

    Our Malai Tikka is a creamy, mild-flavored variant, with marinated chicken or beef cooked in a rich, yogurt-based marinade. It’s succulent, tender, and perfect for those who prefer a smoother taste.

  • Spicy Tikka

    If you love heat, our Spicy Tikka will give you the perfect kick! Made with extra chili and hot spices, it’s a bold and flavorful choice for spice lovers.

  • Seekh Tikka

    Seekh Tikka is a unique twist on the classic, made with minced meat shaped on skewers and grilled until perfectly charred. It’s smoky, juicy, and packed with flavor.

  • Vegetarian Tikka

    Our Veg Tikka is a delightful vegetarian option made with marinated vegetables like paneer, mushrooms, and peppers, grilled to perfection. It’s packed with flavors and spices.
