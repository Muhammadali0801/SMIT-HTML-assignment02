Zaika House Sajji

Enjoy the smoky, tender goodness of our Sajji varieties!

  • Chicken Sajji

    Our Chicken Sajji is a whole chicken marinated in a blend of spices, stuffed with rice, and slow-cooked to perfection. The result is a tender, flavorful chicken with aromatic rice, a traditional Pakistani favorite.

  • Mutton Sajji

    Mutton Sajji is a whole mutton leg, marinated with special spices, stuffed with rice, and roasted until crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside. It’s a hearty, flavorful dish perfect for special occasions.

  • Beef Sajji

    Beef Sajji is a delicious twist on the traditional Sajji, made with marinated beef, stuffed with spiced rice, and slow-cooked to perfection. It’s tender, juicy, and packed with bold flavors.

  • Stuffed Sajji

    This variation of Sajji involves stuffing the whole chicken or mutton with a blend of seasoned rice, nuts, and dried fruits. The slow-cooking process locks in all the flavors, resulting in a fragrant and aromatic dish.

  • Special Sajji

    Our Special Sajji is made using a mix of aromatic spices and a secret blend of ingredients. Whether chicken or mutton, it is slow-cooked over a charcoal grill for that perfect smoky flavor.
